Rack-and-Pinion System

A reliable motion system for precise sinusoidal movement.

Technical Overview

This 3D-printed rack-and-pinion system provides sinusoidal motion, demonstrating the potential of mechanical precision in wearable robotics.

Key Features

  • Sinusoidal motion for rhythmic applications.
  • Lightweight 3D-printed materials.
  • Durable design for long-term use.

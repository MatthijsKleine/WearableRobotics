Robotic Mask

An expressive robotic mask utilizing advanced mechanical systems.

Technical Overview

The robotic mask employs wire-linked mechanisms to create dynamic facial movements. Designed for real-time adaptability, it is perfect for exploring the intersection of mechanics and identity.

Key Features

  • Wire-linked parts for fluid, dynamic expressions.
  • Real-time control for adaptive performance.
  • Lightweight and ergonomic design for extended use.

Created by Matthijs Kleine for the Wearable Robotics Toolkit Project.

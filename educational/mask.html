Robotic Mask

A platform for understanding identity expression through robotics.

Educational Overview

This robotic mask demonstrates how movement systems can be used to convey emotions and identity. Its wire-linked parts offer an engaging way to teach mechanical motion and expressive design.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the principles of wire-linked motion.
  • Explore how robotics can be used to express human emotions.
  • Analyze the design considerations for wearable systems.

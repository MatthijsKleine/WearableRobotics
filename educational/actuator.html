Linear Actuator

Discover the basics of hydraulics and motion control in robotics.

Educational Overview

The linear actuator connected to a syringe demonstrates how hydraulics can generate smooth, controlled motion. This system is a valuable tool for teaching the basics of fluid mechanics in robotics.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the principles of low-pressure hydraulics.
  • Explore the role of actuators in wearable devices.
  • Learn how fluid mechanics enable lifelike motion.

Educational Resources on Wearable Robotics

Learn how wearable robotics can inspire education by integrating mechanics, identity, and cultural applications.

About This Toolkit

This educational-themed toolkit is designed to guide learners and educators in exploring the fascinating world of wearable robotics. By providing clear explanations and examples, it offers a structured approach to understanding the science behind wearable devices and their potential applications in various contexts.

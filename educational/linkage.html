Four-Bar Linkage

Understand periodic motion through compact linkage systems.

Educational Overview

This 3D-printed four-bar linkage demonstrates periodic motion using universal joints. It provides a hands-on way to explore synchronized motion in mechanical systems.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the mechanics of four-bar linkage systems.
  • Explore periodic motion and its applications.
  • Analyze the role of universal joints in robotics.

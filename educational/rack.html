Rack-and-Pinion System

An introduction to generating sinusoidal motion through robotics.

Educational Overview

This 3D-printed rack-and-pinion mechanism demonstrates how sinusoidal motion is generated. It serves as a key learning tool for understanding rhythmic mechanical systems.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the design of rack-and-pinion systems.
  • Analyze how sinusoidal motion is applied in robotics.
  • Learn about 3D printing in prototyping mechanical systems.

Wearable Tentacle

An educational tool to explore fluid and lifelike motion mechanics.

Educational Overview

The wearable tentacle provides a hands-on way to understand motion systems in robotics. Using carbon rods and a winch system, this prototype demonstrates how natural movement can be replicated.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the mechanics behind fluid motion.
  • Explore the use of carbon rods for lightweight yet durable designs.
  • Examine the integration of winch systems for precision control.

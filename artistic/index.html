About This Toolkit

This artistic-themed toolkit explores the intersection of technology and creativity. It is designed to inspire and guide artists in using wearable robotics to enhance their performance art. From masks that convey emotions to dynamic movements powered by innovative actuators, this toolkit provides the tools and insights needed to bring your artistic vision to life.

Prototypes and Features

A lightweight tentacle made from carbon rods and a winch system, enabling fluid, natural movements for storytelling and abstract performances.

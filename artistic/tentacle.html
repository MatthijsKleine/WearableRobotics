Wearable Tentacle

A fluid motion system inspired by natural tentacles, crafted for artistic storytelling.

Prototype Overview

The wearable tentacle is a dynamic device made from carbon rods, a winch system, and 3D-printed connectors. Its fluid and lifelike motion makes it ideal for performances that emphasize movement and transformation.

Key Features

  • Lightweight carbon rod construction for seamless motion.
  • Winch system ensures controlled, precise movements.
  • Customizable design with 3D-printed connectors for flexibility.

Created by Matthijs Kleine for the Wearable Robotics Toolkit Project.

Robotic Mask

A mask that conveys emotions through dynamic movement.

Prototype Overview

Inspired by Tim Hawkinson's work, this robotic mask uses wire-linked parts to create expressive facial movements. It enables performers to convey a range of emotions, enhancing storytelling and audience engagement.

Key Features

  • Wire-linked mechanisms for fluid, expressive movements.
  • Lightweight materials for wearability during performances.
  • Customizable configurations for different expressions.

Created by Matthijs Kleine for the Wearable Robotics Toolkit Project.
